Project

Daily care

In our busy life we are unable to show love and care towards our loved one. For older persons and their families, remembering to take medications on time can be challenging due to factors like forgetfulness, complex medication course. Some common problems include missing doses, mixing up medications, and dealing with side effects. This invention here acts like a medicine reminder is combination of hardware and software, which has some features that are quite helpful to people, no matter who they are. This device consists of buzzer, LED lights, LCD display and touch sensor. To help, setting up reminders through phone app and involving family members or caregivers in medication management can be beneficial.
