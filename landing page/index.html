About

Srisailam, a holy place for Shiva devotees, has great ancient significance.

Srisailam, nestled in the lush Nallamala Hills of Andhra Pradesh, is renowned for its serene beauty and spiritual significance. The Srisailam Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva(mallikarjuna) and Goddess Parvati, is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and a significant Shakti Peetha. The temple's intricate architecture and rich history attract pilgrims and tourists alike. Surrounding the temple, the picturesque landscape of forests and the Krishna River enhance the sense of tranquility. Srisailam offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and spiritual solace, making it a must-visit destination.
